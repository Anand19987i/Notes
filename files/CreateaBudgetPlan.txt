Objective: Develop a monthly budget for a fictional family.

Steps:

Family Profile: Create a fictional family (e.g., two parents, two children).
Income: Estimate total monthly income, considering salaries and any side income.
Expenses: List all potential expenses, such as housing, groceries, transportation, entertainment, and savings.
Spreadsheet: Use Excel or Google Sheets to create a budget template with categories and formulas for totals.
Analysis: Review the budget to ensure it is balanced, identifying areas to cut back if necessary.
Reflection: Write a brief reflection on what the family could do to improve their financial health.
